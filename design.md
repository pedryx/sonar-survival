# Sensor Arena
- player can't see
- he perceive the world only through sonar

# Sonar
- sonar sends waves at regular intervals
- direction rotates in circular pattern
- when wave hit something it reveals it outline

# Arena
- player is enclosed in smaller rectangular arena
- enemies are spawned at regular intervals

# Enemies
- enemies are going in player direction
- when they touch player their outline is revealed and player takes damage

# Outlines
- when outline is revealed it will disappear in a bit

# Player
- player moves WASD and shoots with mouse
- shooting an enemy kills it

# Goal
- Survive as long as you can
